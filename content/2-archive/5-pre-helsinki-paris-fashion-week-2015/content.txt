Title: Pre Helsinki Paris Fashion Week 2015

----

Description: 

Juslin Maunula Creative Collaboration presented their work at S/S 2016 Paris Fashion Week. The presentation was held on October 2nd 2015 at the Finnish Embassy as part of the Pre Helsinki event "After White Night". Fashion Collection by Laura Juslin in collaboration with Huang-Ting Yun. Installation and accessories by Lilli Maunula.
