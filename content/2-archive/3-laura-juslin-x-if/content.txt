Title: Laura Juslin x IF

----

Description: 

In 2015 the nordic insurance company IF commissioned Laura Juslin to design a reflective collection of clothing and accessories to raise traffic safety awareness. The project was done in collaboration with Swedish fashion designer Ann-Sofie Back. The collection will be in stores starting November 6th 2015. The reflective installation commissioned for the launch event of the fashion collection was designed and executed by Lilli Maunula.
Photo: Ville Varumo
